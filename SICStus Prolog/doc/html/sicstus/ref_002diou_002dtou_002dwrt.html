4.6.4.1 Writing Terms: the “Write” Predicates

  • write(+Stream, +Term)
  • write(+Term)
  • writeq(+Stream, +Term)
  • writeq(+Term)
  • write_canonical(+Term)
  • write_canonical(+Stream, +Term)
  • write_term(+Stream, +Term, +Options)
  • write_term(+Term, +Options)

write_term/[2,3] is a generalization of the others and provides a number of options. See mpg-ref-write_term.
